A new size and improved battery life

One of the most exciting updates to the Pixel Watch 3 is the introduction of a new 45mm size option, featuring a larger displayi that provides more space for your apps, workouts, and notifications. The original 41mm size is still available for those who prefer a more compact watch.

Display Specifications:

  • 41mm: 1.2-inch AMOLED display
  • 45mm: 1.4-inch AMOLED display (20% larger than Pixel Watch 2)
  • Up to 1000 nits brightness for better outdoor visibility
  • Always-on display option with power-efficient mode
  • Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection

The Pixel Watch 3 features an LTPO AMOLED display technology that enables a variable refresh rate from 1Hz to 60Hz, helping to conserve battery when displaying static content while providing smooth animations when needed.

The display has a pixel density of 320 PPI, providing crisp text and vibrant colors. The enhanced brightness makes it easier to read in bright sunlight, while the always-on display now uses 30% less power than the previous generation.

Battery life has been a major focus for this generation. The Pixel Watch 3 delivers up to 36 hours of battery lifei with normal use – a 30% improvement over the previous model. Enable Battery Saver mode, and you can extend that to an impressive 60 hours, perfect for weekend trips or overnight adventures.

Battery Specifications:

  • 41mm: 306mAh battery
  • 45mm: 420mAh battery
  • Normal use: Up to 36 hours
  • Battery Saver mode: Up to 60 hours
  • GPS workout tracking: Up to 10 hours continuous

The Pixel Watch 3 offers several power modes to help you manage battery life:

Normal Mode: Full functionality with 36 hours of typical use.

Battery Saver: Extends battery life to 60 hours by reducing screen brightness, limiting background activities, and disabling always-on display.

Time Only Mode: When your battery is critically low, the watch can switch to a time-only display that can last for up to 24 additional hours.

Bedtime Mode: Disables the display and notifications during your designated sleep hours to conserve battery.

The Pixel Watch 3 comes with improved fast charging capabilities:

  • 0 to 50% in just 30 minutes
  • 0 to 80% in 45 minutes
  • Full charge in about 80 minutes

The watch uses a magnetic charging cable that's backward compatible with previous Pixel Watch chargers, and it's Qi-certified for use with compatible wireless chargers.

"We focused on addressing the top requests from Pixel Watch users," said Sandeep Waraich, Director of Product Management for Pixel Watch. "Better battery life, a larger size option, and more advanced fitness features were consistently at the top of the list. The Pixel Watch 3 delivers on all three."

Fine-tuned for fitness

Building on Google's acquisition of Fitbit, the Pixel Watch 3 takes fitness trackingi to the next level with improved sensors, more accurate algorithms, and new workout features that help you train smarter, not just harder.

The Pixel Watch 3 supports over 40 workout types with advanced tracking capabilities:

  • Auto-workout detection for running, walking, elliptical, swimming, and cycling
  • Zone training with customized heart rate zones and real-time guidance
  • Improved GPS accuracy with dual-frequency support for more precise route tracking
  • Personal pace training with target pace alerts during runs
  • Workout summaries with detailed metrics and recovery recommendations

New AI-powered features can analyze your movements to provide form feedback and suggest exercises that complement your routine.

Comprehensive health monitoring features include:

  • Enhanced heart rate sensor with 24/7 monitoring and improved accuracy
  • ECG app for on-demand heart rhythm assessments
  • Irregular heart rhythm notifications that can detect signs of atrial fibrillation
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2) monitoring during sleep
  • Stress tracking with guided breathing exercises
  • Skin temperature tracking for cycle and illness detection
  • NEW: Sleep apnea detection through overnight monitoring patterns

The Pixel Watch 3 provides comprehensive sleep insights:

  • Detailed sleep stages (light, deep, REM) with time in each stage
  • Sleep Score that rates your overall sleep quality
  • Sleep consistency metrics to help establish better routines
  • Snore detection using the built-in microphone
  • Smart wake alarm that wakes you during lighter sleep phases
  • Personalized sleep insights with AI-powered recommendations

The new Sleep Profile feature analyzes your sleep patterns over time and identifies your "sleep animal" type with tailored recommendations.

The enhanced heart rate sensor provides more accurate readings during high-intensity workouts, while the new multi-path algorithm helps maintain accurate tracking regardless of how you wear your watch. For runners, the new pace training feature helps you maintain your target pace with gentle haptic feedback when you're going too fast or too slow.

Sleep tracking has also been enhanced with the addition of sleep apnea detectioni, a potentially life-saving feature that can identify signs of this common but often undiagnosed sleep disorder.

Sleep apnea detection is a new health monitoring feature on the Pixel Watch 3 that can identify potential signs of sleep apnea, a serious sleep disorder that causes breathing to repeatedly stop and start during sleep.

This feature has received FDA clearance for detecting moderate to severe cases of sleep apnea, making the Pixel Watch 3 one of the first smartwatches to offer this capability without requiring additional accessories.

The sleep apnea detection system works by combining multiple sensors:

  • Blood oxygen monitoring to detect drops that occur during apnea events
  • Heart rate variability analysis to identify patterns associated with sleep disruptions
  • Accelerometer data to detect movement patterns consistent with apnea
  • Sleep stage tracking to identify unusual sleep patterns

The watch analyzes this data over several nights to build a comprehensive sleep profile before providing any notifications about potential sleep apnea.

Sleep apnea affects an estimated 22 million Americans, with 80% of moderate to severe cases going undiagnosed. If left untreated, sleep apnea can contribute to serious health problems:

  • High blood pressure
  • Heart disease
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Stroke risk
  • Daytime fatigue

Note: The Pixel Watch sleep apnea detection feature is not intended to replace professional medical diagnosis. Users who receive notifications should consult with healthcare providers for proper evaluation.

Wear OS 5 brings numerous improvements to the Pixel Watch 3:

  • 30% faster app loading times compared to Wear OS 4
  • Redesigned quick settings for easier access to commonly used functions
  • Enhanced notification handling with smart grouping and priority features
  • More customizable watch faces with improved complications
  • Battery optimization for longer life and better performance

The Pixel Watch 3 incorporates AI features that make it more helpful:

  • Smart replies that learn from your communication style
  • Proactive suggestions based on your schedule and activities
  • Fitness insights that adapt to your training patterns
  • Voice typing with on-device processing for faster, more accurate text input
  • AI-powered battery management that adapts to your usage patterns

Seamless integration with Google services:

  • Google Assistant with enhanced on-device capabilities
  • Google Maps with turn-by-turn navigation and offline capabilities
  • Google Wallet for contactless payments and passes
  • Gmail with smart reply suggestions
  • Calendar with proactive reminders and schedule suggestions
  • YouTube Music for on-the-go streaming and offline playlists

Integration with your Pixel phone is smoother than ever, with features like Camera Control letting you use your watch as a remote shutter, Find My Device helping you locate your lost phone (or your watch from your phone), and call handoff that lets you start a call on your watch and seamlessly transfer it to your phone.

Is it worth the upgrade?

If you're currently using the original Pixel Watch, the upgrade to the Pixel Watch 3 is substantial – better battery life, faster performance, and significantly improved health and fitness features make it a worthwhile consideration. For Pixel Watch 2 owners, the calculus is a bit more nuanced: the larger size option, sleep apnea detection, and battery improvements are welcome, but may not be compelling enough for everyone to upgrade just one generation later.

For Android users who haven't yet entered the smartwatch market, particularly those with Pixel phones, the Pixel Watch 3 represents Google's most complete wearable offering to date. The combination of refined hardware, Wear OS 5, and Fitbit's fitness expertise delivers a well-rounded experience that can help you stay connected, active, and healthy.

The Pixel Watch 3 will be available in 27 countries worldwide, with pre-orders starting today at the Google Store and major retailers.
